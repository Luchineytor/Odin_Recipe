Description

The Italian Mule is a variation of the classic Moscow Mule, but with an Italian twist. Itâ€™s made by replacing the traditional vodka with Limoncello, a sweet Italian lemon liqueur, for a refreshing, citrus-forward flavor.

Ingredients

  • Limoncello 2oz
  • Lime Juice 1oz
  • Ginger Beer 4oz
  • Lime slice or mint for garnish

Preparation

  1. Fill a mule mug with ice cubes
  2. Add Limoncello
  3. Add lime juice
  4. Top with ginger beer
  5. Stir gently
  6. Garnish
