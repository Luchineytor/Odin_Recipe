Description

The mojito is a refreshing Cuban cocktail made with white rum, fresh mint leaves, lime juice, sugar, and soda water. It balances sweet, tangy, and herbal flavors for a light, invigorating drink. Traditionally served over ice, it's a perfect choice for warm weather.

Ingredients

  • White Rum 2oz
  • Fresh mint leaves 10u
  • Lime juice 1oz
  • Sugar 1tsp
  • Soda water to top
  • Ice cubes
  • Decorated with lime slice and mint leaves

Peparation steps

  1. Muddle mint, sugar and lime juice
  2. Add whithe rum
  3. Add ice cubes
  4. Top with soda water
  5. Mix gently
  6. Garnish with lime slice and mint leaves
