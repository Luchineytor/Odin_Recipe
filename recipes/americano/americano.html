Description

The Americano is a classic cocktail made with equal parts Campari, sweet vermouth, and a splash of soda water. It's a refreshing, bittersweet drink, typically served over ice with an orange twist or lemon peel garnish. The cocktail originated in Italy and is a lighter, less alcoholic variation of the Negroni.

Ingredients

  • Campari 1oz
  • Sweet Vermouth 1oz
  • Soda water
  • Orange twist or lemon peel for garnish

Preparation

  1. Fill a old fashion glas with ice cubes
  2. Pour the Campari
  3. Add sweet Vermouth
  4. Top with soda water
  5. Stir gently
  6. Garnish
