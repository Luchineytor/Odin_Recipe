Garibaldi

Description

The Garibaldi cocktail is a simple, yet flavorful Italian drink that combines Campari with fresh orange juice, creating a vibrant, bittersweet, and refreshing drink. Itâ€™s named after Giuseppe Garibaldi, an Italian nationalist hero, and is often enjoyed as a pre-dinner aperitif.
Ingredients
  • Campari 2oz
  • Orange juice 4oz
  • Orange slice for garnish

Preparation steps

  1. Fill a glass with ice
  2. Add Campari
  3. Top with orange juice
  4. Stir gently
  5. Garnish with an orange slice
