Description

The margarita is a classic cocktail made with tequila, lime juice, and orange liqueur (typically triple sec or Cointreau). It is often served with a salted rim and can be enjoyed shaken, blended with ice (frozen), or on the rocks. Its bright, tangy, and slightly sweet flavor makes it a popular choice for refreshing drinks.

Ingredients

  • Tequila 2 oz
  • Triple sec 1 oz
  • Lime Juice 1 oz
  • Ice cubes
  • Decorated with lime slice

Preparation

  1. In a cocktail shaker, combine tequila, triple sec, lime juice and ice
  2. Shake vigorously 15-20 sec or intil de shaker condense
  3. Fill a glass with ice and strain th cocktail into the glass
  4. Garnish
